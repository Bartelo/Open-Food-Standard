**GET

curl localhost:3000/things
curl localhost:3000/things/777
curl localhost:3000/things?name=fish

**POST

---- with no properties
curl localhost:3000/things \
-X POST \
-F "thing[name]=testing" 


---- with properties
curl localhost:3000/things \
-X POST \
-F "thing[name]='testing again'"
-F "thing[properties]="
